Pan seared chicken breast

Description

Pan seared chicken breast with rosemary and garlic.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Rosemary
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • Butter
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Season your chicken breast with salt and pepper. If it's too thick, consider butterflying it.
  2. Crush and peel several garlic cloves
  3. Turn the heat up to medium-high and add olive oil to the pan. Lay chicken in and away from you.
  4. Get a nice sear on both sides of the chicken breast
  5. Once seared, turn the heat to medium and add butter, rosemary, and garlic
  6. Baste the chicken until cooked
  7. Take it off the pan and let it rest before cutting
  8. Squeeze some lemon juice on it
