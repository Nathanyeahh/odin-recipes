French Omelette

Description

Slightly more challenging recipe, but not too difficult! It's a bit more technical than the other recipes on my page, but it's a good test of skill in the kitchen according to chefs.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • Parsley
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Crack and whisk 3 eggs in a bowl
  2. Season your eggs before you whisk
  3. After whisked, add some butter to the pan on low-medium heat
  4. Once butter starts to bubble, add your eggs to the pan
  5. After a solid layer has formed on the sides and bottom, start rolling the omelette shape.
  6. Be careful with the temperature of the eggs. You don't want any browning.
  7. Add parsley
