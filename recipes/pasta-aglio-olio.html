Pasta Aglio Olio

Description

Very simple yet tasty pasta recipe perfect for those who have little time or energy but still want to have a delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chili flakes
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Chop garlic into thin slices
  2. Cook your pasta to a perfect al dente
  3. Add olive oil to the pan and cook the garlic slices till lightly brown
  4. Add Chili flakes
  5. Add pasta to the pan with pasta water as needed
  6. Add parsley
  7. Turn the heat to low and let the olive oil and pasta water imulsify
